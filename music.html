Music & Sound Design

Experimental sound works, audio-reactive installations, and composition for media

Selected Releases

20+ albums exploring experimental electronic, ambient, and sound art under Aspen Da'nee / ScilentSymphony, with AI tools used selectively for technical tasks like stem separation

Pristine Inertia

2022

A tightly constructed 30-minute statement of forward motion and technical discipline. Eleven tracks navigate energetic propulsion and melancholic introspection, marking the shift from preset reliance to intentional sound design. The album's cohesion comes from its restraint—every element earns its place.

Spotify YouTube

New Era

2024

Bold, abrasive, and unrelenting. Twenty tracks explore the boundary between chaos and control through industrial textures, hyperpop aggression, and rhythmic complexity. The album interrogates how extreme sound can remain intentionally compelling, anchored by harmonic clarity and strategic density limits.
Spotify YouTube

Nothing New

2025

An hour-long recomposition built entirely from past work. Using only stems and samples from previous albums, seventeen tracks transform history into something seamless and forward-moving. The challenge: create cohesion from constraint, proving that reinvention doesn't require new materials—only new perspective.
Spotify YouTube
