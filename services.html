Common Questions

Project timelines vary based on scope. Short-form work typically takes 1-2 weeks, while larger projects are scheduled based on mutual availability. Rush options may be available.

I primarily work remotely but am available for in-person collaboration in the Southwest Florida area. Travel for specific projects can be discussed.

What if I'm not sure which service I need?

That's what the discovery call is for! We'll discuss your goals and I'll recommend the best approach, whether that's a single service or a combination.

Yes, discounted rates are available for academic researchers, students, and registered non-profit organizations. Inquire for details.
